# Adding a Client

---

From the Clients dashboard page, click the “NEW CLIENT” button. This will open a popup.

![Screenshot of the New Client button](/assets/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/new-client-button.png)

Add the Client name, and choose the Financial Period for which the Client will be submitting a tax return. Click “Create” and the new Client will be added to the Clients dashboard.

![Screenshot of the new client popup](/assets/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/new-client-modal-1024x636.png)
