# Editing categories

---

To edit a category click the pencil icon next to the Category title.

![](/assets/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/edit-category-icon.png)

Then make your edit in the popup and click “Save”.

![](/assets/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/edit-category-modal-1024x366.png)

To delete a category click the bin icon next to the category title.

![](/assets/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/delete-category.png)
