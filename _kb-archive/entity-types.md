# Entity types

---

A taxable entity is any person, company or trust for which a tax return must be lodged. There are three types of entity – Individual, Company, Trust.

A client can have more than one entity. For example a client might have two Individual entities (themselves and their spouse’s returns), a Company, and a Trust.

Each Entity type has it’s own Catalogue of categories or questions and requirements.
