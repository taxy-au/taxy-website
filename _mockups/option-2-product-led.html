FAQs

Frequently asked questions.

These come up most. Looking for more? FAQs for accountants · FAQs for clients.

What is Taxy?

Taxy helps accounting firms structure, send and track client information requests for tax jobs.

Clients receive clear requests in one place; your team can immediately see what's been provided and what's still outstanding.

How is Taxy different from email or a client portal?

Email threads and portals often become a dumping ground for documents and questions. Taxy structures requests so clients know exactly what's required and teams can quickly track progress.

How long does it take to get started?

Most firms send their first request within a few days. Many begin with a single multi-entity client group to see how Taxy fits alongside their existing workflow.

Will my clients actually use it?

Most firms are surprised by how quickly clients respond when requests are clear and organised.

Is my data secure in Taxy?

Yes. Information is encrypted, stored in Australia, and only accessible to authorised users in your workspace.
