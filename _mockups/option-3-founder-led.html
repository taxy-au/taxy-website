Why Taxy exists

We built Taxy because tax work shouldn't feel like chasing.

Every accounting firm we spoke with told the same story. Spreadsheets. Email threads. Client portals nobody logs into. Tax season turned into a job of chasing — and the people doing it were the most senior, most expensive people in the firm.

So we built the tool we wished existed. Structured requests, visible progress, year-round. Built specifically for Australian firms — not adapted from something built for somewhere else.

If that resonates, talk to us. We'll set it up for you, run it alongside your existing process, and you'll see whether it fits.

Amanda — Co-founder, Taxy

FAQs

Frequently asked questions.

These come up most. Looking for more? FAQs for accountants · FAQs for clients.

What is Taxy?

Taxy helps accounting firms structure, send and track client information requests for tax jobs. Clients receive clear requests in one place; your team can see what's been provided and what's still outstanding.

How is Taxy different from email or a client portal?

Email threads and portals often become a dumping ground for documents and questions. Taxy structures requests so clients know exactly what's required and teams can quickly track progress.

How long does it take to get started?

Most firms send their first request within a few days. Many begin with a single multi-entity client group to see how Taxy fits alongside their existing workflow.

Will my clients actually use it?

Most firms are surprised by how quickly clients respond when requests are clear and organised.

Is my data secure in Taxy?

Yes. Information is encrypted, stored in Australia, and only accessible to authorised users in your workspace.
