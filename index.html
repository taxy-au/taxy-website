What is Taxy?

Taxy helps accounting firms structure, send and track client information requests for tax jobs.

Clients receive clear requests in one place, and your team can immediately see what has been provided and what is still outstanding.

 

How is Taxy different from using email or a client portal?

Email threads and portals often become a dumping ground for documents and questions, making it hard to see what’s still missing.

Taxy structures requests so clients know exactly what’s required and teams can quickly track progress without digging through emails.

 

How long does it take to get started?

Customers typically send their first request within a few days. Many begin with a single job or multi-entity client group to see how Taxy fits alongside their existing workflow.

 

Will my clients actually use it?

Most firms are surprised by how quickly clients respond when requests are clear and organised.

Clients can see exactly what’s needed, upload documents or responses in one place, and work through requests at their own pace.

 

Is my data secure in Taxy?

Yes. Security is core to Taxy.

All information is encrypted, stored in Australia, and only accessible to authorised users in your workspace.
